Yue Gao (高 月)
yuegao@microsoft.com

I am currently a researcher of Media Computing Group at Microsoft Research Asia, working closely with Fangyun Wei, Yan Lu, Xiang Ming, Yuan Zhou and Jianmin Bao.

Under the guidance of Professor Zhouhui Lian, I obtained a master's degree in Computer Application Technology at Peking University. Prior to that, I earned a bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Beijing Institute of Technology.

My research interests lie in computer vision and computer graphics. Specifically, I am interested in generative models, 3D reconstruction, video processing and self-supervised learning.
