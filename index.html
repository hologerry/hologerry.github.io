I am currently a researcher at Microsoft Research Asia.

Under the guidance of Professor Zhouhui Lian, I obtained a master's degree in Computer Applied Technology at Peking University.

My research interests lie in computer vision and computer graphics. Specifically, I am interested in generative models, dynamics models, video processing and generation, as well as self-supervised learning.
